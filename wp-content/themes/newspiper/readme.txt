== Description ==

Newspiper is a contemporary WordPress theme for blogs, news and magazines. It is specifically tailored for bloggers, content creators and independent publishers. The theme has generous theme options and features beautiful block patterns to help you get started in minutes. Newspiper is optimized for speed, cross-browser compatible and mobile friendly. It is also SEO-optimized and uses Schema.org markup (microdata) to help you rank higher in Google search results. The theme is highly customizable and easy to adapt to any design. You can easily add social media links in the header for Facebook, Twitter, TikTok, Instagram, LinkedIn, Youtube, Twitch, Flickr, Pinterest, Snapchat and many more. The theme is specally designed to make your content stand out from the crowd and makes it easy for you to promote your content through affiliate programs or Google AdSense. It also provides effortless integration with popular WordPress plugins such as WooCommerce, Paid Memberships Pro and Mailchimp for WordPress that will help you gather subscribers and put content behind a paywall. Protect your visitors' eyes by allowing them to switch between day and night mode (light and dark mode) with a single click. Oh, and just like with our other themes, updates will keep on coming, constantly improving the codebase and keeping it up to date to the modern web standards. Still not convinved? Check the theme demo at https://newspiper-demo.nasiothemes.com/ Curious to test its features? Try it at https://newspiper-demo.nasiothemes.com/test-drive/

== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance > Themes and click the Add Newspiper button.
2. Click Upload Theme and Choose File, then select the theme's .zip file. Click Install Now.
3. Click Activate to use your newspiper theme right away.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Does this theme support any plugins? =

Newspiper includes support for WooCommerce and for Infinite Scroll in Jetpack.
